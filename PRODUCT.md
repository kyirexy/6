# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese-speaking users (primarily mobile) who watch long-form video content on Douyin/Bilibili and want to retain knowledge efficiently. They are productivity-minded, tech-comfortable, and prefer quick consumption over watching full videos.

## Product Purpose

VideoCapsule converts long videos into structured knowledge cards. Users paste a video URL, the app extracts the transcript via ASR, and AI generates a beautifully formatted card with sections, conclusions, and a pitfall rating. Success: users can understand a 30-minute video's key points in under 2 minutes.

## Brand Personality

- **Efficient**: No fluff, every element serves a purpose
- **Dark-tech**: Glassmorphism dark theme with emerald accents, feels like a hacker tool
- **Delightful**: Micro-interactions and smooth transitions that reward usage

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with blue/white color schemes
- Overly playful or childish UI with rounded bubbles
- Cluttered news-aggregator layouts
- Any design that looks like a WordPress template

## Design Principles

1. **Content-first**: The knowledge card is the hero; chrome is minimal
2. **Mobile-native**: Touch targets, responsive, PWA-ready
3. **Dark elegance**: Glassmorphism done right - purposeful, not decorative
4. **Instant feedback**: Every action has immediate visual response
5. **Accessible contrast**: 4.5:1 minimum for body text

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- prefers-reduced-motion support
- 44px minimum touch targets on mobile
- High contrast mode support
